# **App Name**: ViralBoost AI

## Core Features:

- Social Account Integration: Allow users to connect their Instagram and TikTok accounts via their respective APIs to pull key metrics.
- Content Calendar Generation: Generate a weekly content calendar based on the user's niche, goals, and posting frequency. Suggests the best platform for each day.
- Video Idea Generation: Proactively suggest video ideas based on the user's niche and current trends, providing a basic script outline for each idea.
- Pre-Publication Video Analysis: Analyze user-submitted videos for common issues that reduce retention, such as a weak hook, poor technical quality, or slow pace, and then suggests improvements. This will act as a 'tool' to aid the decision process on the video before posting
- Trend Feed Aggregation: Aggregate and display trending audio, dances, and video formats, filtering by the user's niche to show relevant trends.
- Monetization Assistant: Generate a professional media kit with key metrics and suggest pricing for different types of advertising based on the user's stats and niche.
- Sponsored Content Idea Generator: Generate creative and authentic sponsored content ideas based on the received product and the user's niche, suggesting the best format for conversions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated blue (#29ABE2) to evoke a sense of trust, reliability, and forward-thinking.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue (#E5F6FD), a muted and soft background provides a visually calming space for users, making the content pop and the interface feel modern and approachable.
- Accent color: Vibrant green (#90EE90) to highlight calls to action and positive feedback, this color is far from primary in brightness and saturation so that contrast remains legible and dynamic
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif font to provide a contemporary and fashionable feel. Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif to create a neutral reading experience
- Use clear, modern icons that represent the different features and actions within the app.
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout with a focus on mobile-first design principles.
- Incorporate subtle animations to provide feedback and guide the user through the app, making it a pleasant and interactive experience.
